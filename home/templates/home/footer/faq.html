FAQ

Who are we?

Founded in 2019 by a 'couple' of world travellers! With a passion for adventure, the pair set out to find the best hidden gems. From food and drink, to culture, to the best beaches and wildlife. We've got you covered!

What makes myway stand out from other travel companies?

We cater to your taste of adventure! We personalise your trip to suit your desires. All ages are welcome, and we encourage groups with something to celebrate, from Hen nights to 70th birthdays!

How much would I roughly spend on a trip?

Due to the variety of trips we have, the prices may vary depending on location, length of trip and how many friends are in your group!

Is this safe for somebody travelling solo?

Solo travellers were who we had in mind when creating myway! Although you would be "solo", you would be surrounded by like mind individuals, seeking adventure as well! Meeting friends along the way! We also have our group leaders who are there to ensure everybody feels safe and included!

How many people will be on each trip?

The amount of people varies depending on which tour you choose! We do have a maximum number of people per trip, to ensure each individual and group get the best experience possible! When booking your trip you will see a maximum occupancy attached with the tour info
