Code of Conduct

Your promise as a traveller with myway

Travelling with myway is about embracing local cultures, living authentic experiences and making life-long friends. However, bringing our travellers on an adventure of a lifetime isn't possible unless we all show respect for the places we visit and for one another.

Whilst we offer freedom on all of our trips, we have a few rules that must be followed:

  • Be flexible and understanding of timings and itineraries.
  • Respect local customs and cultural values.
  • Be polite, kind and respectful to trip managers, trip drivers, locals, fellow travellers and anyone else you might encounter.
  • Be aware that certain words or phrases that might be commonplace in your home countries are offensive in others and adapt your language for the situation you are in.
  • Leave the place as you found it.

Myway has a Zero Tolerance Policy to the following acts and reserves the right to terminate your trip on the grounds of the following behaviour:

  • Drugs: Possession and use of recreational drugs and illegal substances at myway-associated properties and on our coaches and other private transport is strictly prohibited and will result in the immediate removal from trip.
  • Alcohol: Excess alcohol consumption that puts travellers and crew at risk is grounds for removal from the trip. Please drink responsibly and look after yourself.
  • Abusive behaviour: Under no circumstances will physical violence of any type be tolerated on our trips or within any myway-associated facility.
  • Verbal Abuse: myway will not tolerate travellers that use abusive language or make gestures that could be disrespectful or threatening. Inappropriate conduct that affects those around you, whether a fellow traveller, myway representative or business partner is unacceptable.
Any threats to safety, well-being or inappropriate behaviour may lead to the immediate termination of a traveller's trip. Travellers removed from a trip for failure to comply to the Myway Traveller Code of Conduct are responsible for repatriation and related costs and have no claims against myway.
